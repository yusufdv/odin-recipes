Odin recipes

  • CousCous

    • Algeria’s most iconic dish, Couscous is made from steamed semolina grains and served with tender meat, vegetables, and a spiced broth. Whether enjoyed with lamb, chicken, or vegetables only, this comforting classic brings families together every Friday and on special occasions.

  • Kbda Mchrmla

    • A bold and flavorful Algerian specialty made with tender lamb liver cooked in a rich chermoula sauce. Seasoned with garlic, cumin, paprika, and a hint of lemon, Kebda Mchermla is a beloved dish known for its deep taste and hearty texture—perfect for true lovers of traditional cuisine.

  • Rchta

    • A festive favorite, Rechta features delicate handmade noodles served with a light and aromatic white sauce made from chicken, chickpeas, and turnips. Infused with cinnamon and onions, this dish is especially enjoyed during religious holidays and family gatherings.
