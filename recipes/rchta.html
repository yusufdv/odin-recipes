Rechta

What Is Rechta?

Rechta is a traditional Algerian dish made of fine handmade noodles served in a white sauce with chicken, chickpeas, and turnips. It’s especially popular on religious holidays and special occasions.

Ingrediants

  • Rechta noodles (fresh or dry)
  • Chicken pieces
  • Chickpeas
  • Turnips
  • Onions
  • Oil or smen (fermented butter)
  • Spices like cinnamon, black pepper, and salt
  • optional: carrots or zucchini for variation

Preparing Steps:

  1. Prepare the sauce
    In a pot, cook chopped onions, chicken, chickpeas, and spices in oil or smen. Let them sauté until lightly golden.
  2. Add vegetables and water
    Add turnips and enough water to cover everything. Simmer until the chicken and vegetables are cooked through.
  3. Steam the rechta noodles
    Place the noodles in a steamer. Steam them once, remove and fluff, then steam again until tender and light.
  4. Serve
    Place the noodles on a serving plate. Top with the sauce, chicken, and vegetables. Serve hot.
  5. Optional garnish
    Add a touch of parsley or extra smen on top before serving.

Why You'll Love It:

Rechta is comforting, flavorful, and filled with tradition. Its soft noodles and rich broth make it a favorite for both everyday meals and festive gatherings.
